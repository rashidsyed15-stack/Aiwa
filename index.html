Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our recruitment process.

Visa processing time varies by country and employer. Typically, it can take anywhere from 2 to 6 weeks after the offer letter is signed and all documents are submitted.

Candidates must undergo a medical examination at a GAMCA-approved medical center. We provide full assistance in scheduling appointments and guiding you through the process.

Salaries are based on your profession, experience, qualifications, and the specific country's market standards. We negotiate for the best possible packages for our candidates.

Initially, you need an updated CV/Resume. After shortlisting, you will need your passport, educational certificates, experience letters, and photographs. We will provide a detailed checklist.
